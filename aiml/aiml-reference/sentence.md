### &lt;sentence&gt;

The *sentence element* capitalizes the first word of its contents, as if the contents were the beginning of a sentence.

#### Usage

    <category>
      <pattern>A B C</pattern>
      <template><sentence><input/></template>
    </category>

>**Input:** a b c  
**Output:** A b c
